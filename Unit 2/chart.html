High disparities amongst Staten Island distracted driving accidents

By Christine DeRosa

Since the beginning of 2019, more than 3,000 vehicular accidents have occurred on Staten Island, according to data from the NYPD.

The dataset was created from accidents reported to the NYPD via police reports, which are required to be filled out for collisions where someone is injured or killed, or where there is at least $1000 worth of damage.

Over 800 accidents in the borough were caused by distracted driving. Hand-held cell phone use was not the main cause of these accidents.
